Mission: Our Mission/Aim is to educate peoples in the fullest sense. After they leave school they should be confident, & have the ability to express themselves clearly. They should also have a well-developed sense of right & wrong, a sense of duty & a belief in their own worth. The staff has a long tradition of encouraging pupils to discover their gifts & abilities in a secure & happy environment. We regard our students as young adults & involve them in decisions concerning their work & school life. Our School is a house between the studies & life outside school. We provide an environment that helps our students grow individually. Many find this environment essential to develop or build-up their potential & career.
